About Param Computer Centre

6 years of commitment to quality education and community digital services in Ludhiana.

📖 Who We Are

Building Skills,
Shaping Futures

Param Computer Centre was established with one mission — making quality computer education accessible to every student and individual in our community. Located at Pritam Colony, 33 Feet Road, Giaspura, Ludhiana, we have served thousands of people over 6+ years.

Whether you're a student looking for your first job, a homemaker wanting to learn basics, or a professional upgrading skills — Param Computer Centre is the right place for you.

Beyond education, we also serve as a community digital centre — Aadhar, PAN, banking, PF and government form assistance for thousands of residents every year.

🕰️ Our Journey

A Legacy of 6 Years

1

2018 – Foundation

Param Computer Centre opened in Giaspura with basic computer courses and a vision to serve the community.

2

2019–2020 – Expansion

Added DCA and ADCA programs. Tally ERP 9 course launched. Grew to 200+ students per year.

3

2021 – Digital Services

Launched banking CSP, Aadhar and PAN card services to serve local community needs.

4

2022–2023 – Growth

GST added to Tally curriculum. Advance Excel launched. Crossed 700+ total students trained.

5

2024 – Present

1000+ students trained. Trusted centre for education and digital services in Ludhiana.
